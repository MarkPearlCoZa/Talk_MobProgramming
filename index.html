Mob Programming is...


When three or more people work together to solve a problem

at one computer!


- it's being done all over the world -

What it looks like...


Roles in Mob Programming...

  • The typist
  • The rest of the mob

The typist

  1. Understand what the mob is asking you to do
  2. Translate it to code
  3. Work with an incomplete understanding for short periods of time

The rest of the mob

  1. Help discover the next logical step
  2. Talk at appropriate levels
  3. Always try to increase levels of understanding
